What I do now

I lead data science at CVS Media Exchange (CMX), building agentic marketing and retail media at the scale of a Fortune 6. My team designs AI agents that run campaigns end-to-end — analyzing real-time signals, adapting to consumer behavior, and pushing measurement, targeting, and audience automation past where traditional martech stops. The launch of CorIQ™, our AI-powered media platform built on ExtraCare® data, is the most recent product of that 18-month vision.

Earlier in my CVS run I built the personalization and recommendation engine for CVS.com, serving over 2.5 billion visitors, and helped install a real culture of experimentation across the org — making "test and learn" the default, not a side project.

How I got here

Before data science

I came in through the side door. PhD in Physical Chemistry from Arizona State University (2015) — dissertation on molecular models for conductance in junctions and electrochemical electron transfer; M.Sc. in Physical Chemistry from Sharif University of Technology. Research spanned nickel catalysts for hydrogen production (with Dr. Jones' group, published in JACS) and non-equilibrium Green's-function transport in molecular wires. On the side I built AstroPaint, an open-source Python package for painting astrophysical signals onto sky maps. The quantum/astro detour shaped how I think about uncertainty, models, and what it means for a result to be "real."
